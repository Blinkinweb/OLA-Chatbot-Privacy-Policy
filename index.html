OLA Messenger Bot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

OLA Messenger Bot ("the Service") is a Facebook Messenger automation system that allows users to interact with automated tasks, account features, and reward systems through the OLA Facebook Page.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when users interact with the chatbot and how that information is used and protected.

By using this Messenger bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When users interact with the Messenger bot, the system may collect limited information required to operate the service.

  • Facebook Page-Scoped ID (PSID)
  • User interaction data (messages, quick replies, button clicks)
  • Account activity data (task progress, balances, rewards)
  • Activation code usage associated with the account

The service does not collect sensitive personal information such as:

  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Government identification
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers

3. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used strictly to operate the chatbot service and its core functionality.

  • Identifying users interacting with the system
  • Maintaining account progress and balances
  • Processing tasks and reward logic
  • Preventing spam or misuse of the service
  • Providing technical support and troubleshooting

4. Data Storage and Security

Some operational data may be stored in a secure database in order to maintain account progress, balances, and system integrity.

Reasonable technical measures are implemented to protect stored data from unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Data Sharing

The service does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Information collected through the Messenger bot is used solely to provide the functionality of the service.

6. Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the service and maintain account functionality.

Inactive or deleted accounts may have their data removed periodically.

7. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

To request deletion, send the message:

DELETE ACCOUNT

to the Messenger bot associated with this service.

Once received, the request will be processed and the associated data will be removed from the system within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Facebook Platform Compliance

This service operates through the Facebook Messenger Platform and complies with Facebook Platform Policies.

Information exchanged through Messenger interactions may also be subject to Facebook's own privacy policies.

You can review Facebook’s privacy policy here:

https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or operational changes to the service.

Updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data requests, please contact the administrator through the official Facebook Page associated with this Messenger bot.
